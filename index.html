Operational Excellence

Wood Pellet Production Process

1

Sourcing & Preparation

Raw material (Acacia, Eucalyptus) undergoes debarking (up to 90% purity) and chipping into uniform fiber.

2

Refinement & Drying

Hammer mills pulverize wood into fine material, while magnetic separators remove impurities before entering high-efficiency dryers.

3

Precision Pelletizing

Fine material is extruded through heavy-duty pelletizing dies to create high-density, energy-rich pellets.

4

Cooling & Global Export

Final pellets are cooled, screened for quality, and shipped globally via major regional ports.
